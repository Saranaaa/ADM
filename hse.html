HSE

Our company is responsible for the health and safety of our employees and protection of the environment as prescribed by the Law.

The following applies to all employees:

1. All accidents and incidents are preventable and should be considered while planning any activity;

2. Leadership is responsible for proper HSE management

3. Everyone should participate in promoting Safe operations;

4. HSE is just as important as any other activity;

5. It is important to perform work in accordance with policies, standards, procedures, rules and guidelines;

6. All risks must be reduced to an acceptable level;

7. Elimination of risky actions and inactions is vital ;

8. Demonstrate commitment to HSE leadership;

9. An HSE success must be included in working plan;

10. Work must be completed in a healthy, safe, reliable and environmentally friendly manner;

11. Resources for HSE are your secure future;

12. HSE is a 7/24 lifestyle.

Our management principles:

• Implement HSE management systems and processes that meet international and local standards;
• Set HSE goals, analyze the results and work on permanent improvement;
• Meet the technical and organizational requirements to achieve our HSE goals;
• Actively identify, develop and maintain required HSE practices;
• Allocate enough time to assess identified hazards;
• Consider and plan recovery measures to address HSE emergencies, crises and failures;
• Stop work if there is an HSE risk of and continue only when the risk is lowered to acceptable level;
• To grow further consider HSE for past and future;
• Promote and systematically report openly and honestly on non-compliant actions, requests , and events, and identify preventive and control measures to minimize future recurrence as a result of research;
• Actively share and collaborate with employees, contractors, and customers on HSE-related topics;
• Accept and share the success stories as a result of right HSE decisions;
• Monitor compliance with our requirements by conducting audits to support development of HSE activities.

Health, Occupational Safety and Environmental Policy

As a company we are committed:
• For proper implementation of HSE Management system in workplaces to give a visible responsibility;
• To require all staff to take full responsibility for the health and safety of own and others, which shows that we all care about each other as a team in a work environment;
• Consider the environmental impact in our operation to minimizepollution, waste and inefficient use of natural resources.

We succeed where others may fail and finish what we start in best standards. We comply with business integrity and build long-lasting relations. We are committed to the space we operate and prove our reliability through proven records of achievements.
